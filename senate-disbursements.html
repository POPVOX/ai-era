Parsing note

This prototype extracts rows from PDF text and keeps report/page references so records can be checked against the source. PDF extraction can introduce row parsing errors.

The transaction tab loads the largest parsed transactions for browser performance; summary totals are calculated from all parsed rows.

2025 caveat

Transaction rows from overlapping reports are included when posted or service dates fall in 2025. Payroll rows in Senate PDFs do not always include line-level dates, so overlapping report payroll rows are included with a visible report-period caveat.
