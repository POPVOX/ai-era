Co-founder / CEO

Marci Harris

JD/LLM

Marci co-founded POPVOX to make policymaking more transparent, accountable, and usable for people inside and outside government. Her work connects legislative process, civic participation, and technology for democratic institutions.

Read more

As founder and CEO of POPVOX.com and the Executive Director of the nonprofit POPVOX Foundation, Marci is passionate about the responsible use of technology to improve government and benefit humanity. She is a lawyer and former Congressional staffer, who worked on the House Ways and Means Committee's Affordable Care Act team. She has held fellowships with Harvard Kennedy School's Ash Center for Democracy and the New America Foundation, and taught public policy, government, and civic technology courses at the University of San Francisco, San Jose State University, and the University of California Berkeley. She is the author of the forthcoming book RECONSTITUTION: A Blueprint for American Governance in the Age of Acceleration.

Director of Government Innovation

Anne Meeker

Anne bridges the gap between government institutions and modern technology, guiding innovation strategy that respects the complexities of democratic process.

Read more

Anne Meeker is a former congressional casework director. She joined POPVOX, Inc. in 2020 and helped create the nonprofit POPVOX Foundation, where she served in a variety of roles including as Managing Director and created the Foundation's Casework Navigator program. Her testimony before the House Select Committee on the Modernization of Congress in 2024 inspired recommendations that led to the CaseCompass project, an effort to aggregate and organize the House's incoming casework. She regularly advises Congress on casework best practices and incorporating technology into casework workflows.

Anne previously served as a Fellow with OSU's Institute for Democratic Engagement and Accountability. She holds a bachelor's degree in Anthropology from the University of Oxford (St. Hugh's College), and an MSc in History from the London School of Economics. She is also a proud Clevelander, and will take any opportunity to talk up the Rust Belt.
